PBM AI Trading

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 6, 2026

PBM is a market analysis and trading workspace. This policy explains how information is handled when you use the PBM web or Android application.

Information PBM processes

  • Account information, including email address and authentication identifiers.
  • Workspace data you provide, including watchlists, alerts, journals, analysis history, teaching feedback, Social posts and uploaded images.
  • Technical information needed to operate the service, including device push-notification tokens and basic request diagnostics.

How information is used

Information is used to authenticate users, provide personal workspace features, run requested market and AI analyses, deliver notifications, secure the service, and improve PBM through user-provided feedback.

Service providers

PBM uses service providers including Supabase for authentication and data storage, Cloudflare for application delivery, Firebase for Android push notifications, Anthropic for requested AI analysis, market-data providers, and email-delivery providers when email notifications are enabled.

Sharing and retention

PBM does not sell personal information. Data is shared with service providers only as needed to operate requested features. Workspace data is retained while the account is active or as needed to operate and secure the service.

Your choices

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your account and associated data using the account deletion page.

Financial disclaimer

PBM provides analysis and educational tools. It does not provide financial advice and does not guarantee market outcomes.
